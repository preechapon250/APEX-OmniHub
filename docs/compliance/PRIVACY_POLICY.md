# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-01-28

## 1. Introduction

APEX-OmniHub ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

## 2. Information We Collect

- **Account Information**: Name, email, and authentication tokens (via Supabase).
- **Usage Data**: API calls, feature usage, and performance metrics (via OmniSentry).
- **User Content**: Data you explicitly store within the OmniHub platform.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
- To notify you about changes or incidents.
- To prevent fraud and ensure security (Guardian/Triforce protocols).

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted service providers (e.g., Supabase, Vercel, Stripe) solely for the purpose of operating the service.

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

- **Access**: Request a copy of your data.
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate information.
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data ("Right to be Forgotten").
- **Export**: Download your data in a portable format.
